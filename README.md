# GlowBalance ✨

**Split expenses with friends, family, or flatmates and watch your balances glow.**

Bright neon UI, smart settlements, beautiful charts, and zero drama. Built for good vibes only.

Live Demo: [https://glowbalance.netlify.app](https://glowbalance.netlify.app/)

---

## About GlowBalance

GlowBalance is a beautiful and simple expense splitter designed to make shared finances stress-free. Whether you're splitting rent with flatmates, managing trip costs with friends, or tracking daily expenses with your partner — GlowBalance handles the math so you don’t have to.

Add expenses effortlessly, see real-time balances, settle up cleanly, and enjoy insightful visualizations — all wrapped in a vibrant neon aesthetic that makes finance feel fun.

No sign-up. No complexity. Just good vibes and fair splits.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Quick Expense Entry** — Add shared expenses with description, amount, date, payer, and involved members
- **Smart Balance Calculation** — Automatically tracks who owes whom with precise balances
- **Settle Up** — One-click settlement suggestions to clear dues easily
- **Balance Overview** — Crystal-clear view of everyone’s financial standing
- **Beautiful Insights & Charts** — Visual breakdowns of total spending, per-person shares, and trends
- **Neon-Powered UI** — Modern, vibrant, and responsive design that feels premium
- **Mobile Friendly** — Works smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops

## Who is it for?

- Roommates & flatmates splitting bills
- Friends on group trips or outings
- Couples managing household expenses
- Travel buddies and event groups
- Anyone tired of messy group payment calculations
